Alfredo

A classic dish that is quick and easy to make. It can feed one or feed 6 by simply changing the amount of noodles added to the dish.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine Noodles
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Bring your water to a boil.
  2. Add Fettuccine noodles.
  3. Once noodles are at a texture you like, remove them from water.
  4. Add parmesan cheese, alfredo sauce, and oregano to noodles.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
