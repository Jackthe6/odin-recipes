Crab Cakes

Description

This is a seafood classic. It may not be the most beginner friendly recipe but any seafood lover will enjoy this.

Ingredients

  • Lump crab meat
  • Breading
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Mix your lump crab meat and breading together.
  2. Once mixed, add your egg to make sure it sticks together.
  3. Pan fry until golden brown.
