Spaghetti

Description

A classic staple of Italian food, it is the first dish that comes to mind. Made up of spaghetti noodles and maranara sauce with meatballs being optional.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Maranara Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Ground beef for meatballs(optional)

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti noodles.
  3. Cook to desired texture.
  4. Remove noodles and add parmesan and maranara
